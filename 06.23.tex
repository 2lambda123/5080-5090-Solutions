of 6.4.4, $M_{Y}(t) = [M_{X}(t)]^n$ where $Y = X_1 + X_2 + ... + X_n$. then since $X_i \sim GEO(p)$ \\

\begin{eqnarray*}
	Mgf(Y) &=& M_{X_1}(t) M_{X_2}(t) ... M_{X_k}(t) \\
		&=& (M_{X}(t))^k \\
		&=& ( \frac{pe^t}{1-qe^t} )^k \sim NegativeBinomial(k,p) \\
\end{eqnarray*}

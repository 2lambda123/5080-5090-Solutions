

{\bf 08.16} a) We make $\overline{X}$ into standard normal then calculate. \\
\begin{align*}
	\Pr \left( 3 < \overline{X} < 7 \right) & = 
		\Pr \left( \frac{3 - 6}{\frac{5}{3}} < \frac{\overline{X} - 6}{\frac{5}{3}} < \frac{7 - 6}{\frac{5}{3}} \right) \\
	& = \Pr \left( -1.8 < \Phi < .6 \right) \\
	& = \Phi ( .6 ) - \Phi (-1.8) \\
	& \approx .6898 \\
\end{align*}
